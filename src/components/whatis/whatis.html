What is Mindspace?

  • Capture thoughts with a shortcut

    Random brain flashes and great ideas are captured quickly with an easy keyboard shortcut. Just hit ⌃⌥Space.

  • Access all your scribbles at any time

    Pop up the input bar to add, edit or search notes. Start typing and Mindspace instantly shows your existing notes.

  • Customize your experience

    Customize the look and feel, just by manipulating lines of text in the preference note. It’s that easy.
